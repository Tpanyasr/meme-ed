Louisiana Purchase, 1803




Previous Next






The Louisiana Purchase, the western half of the Mississippi River basin purchased in 1803 from France by the United States, was the greatest bargain in U.S history; Thomas Jefferson purchased the land from Napoleon Bonaparte for $15 million, less than three cents per acre for 828,000 square miles (2,144,520 square km)!
